QUESTIONS FOUNDERS ASK

Straight answers.

I'm bootstrapped. Can we still work together?

Yes, if the idea is strong. We've structured engagements with cash + equity blends for founders with real conviction and tight cash. The free idea review is where we figure out the right model for you.

Do I need to know how to code?

No. The entire offer is built for non-technical founders. You bring domain insight and decision-making — we bring the technical build, architecture, and team.

How is this different from hiring an agency?

Agencies ship a deliverable and leave. We ship a company — product, legal, brand, and investor intros — and stay on as long as you need. And unlike an agency, we think like co-founders: we challenge the idea, propose better versions, and own the outcome with you.

What AI work do you actually do?

Production AI systems: LLM-based applications, agentic pipelines, retrieval systems, fine-tuning, voice AI, infrastructure, and cost-optimized deployment. AI-native, not AI-sprinkled.

Who owns the code and IP?

You. 100%. From day one. We don't hold IP, we don't white-label, and we don't lock you into retainers to maintain code we wrote.

What if I already have an MVP?

Start at step 2 or 3 of the journey. We scale what you have, fix what's fragile, and support your investor and customer conversations. You don't restart — you plug us in.

How do investor intros actually work?

We open our network to founders we're engaged with. You pitch; we introduce. We're selective about who we put in front of our investor and customer contacts — our reputation is on the line.

What does a 45-day engagement actually cost?

Every engagement is quoted after the free idea review. Ranges are in the Pricing section. We don't publish fixed prices because no two launches are identical — but we won't surprise you either. Each milestone is priced up front, so you always see the next number before we ship it.

What happens after 45 days?

Most founders continue with us as an ongoing tech partner while they hire their own team. Some hand off entirely. The engagement is month-to-month — no long contracts.

How selective are you?

We take on founders we believe can build something real. The idea review is a two-way qualification: you're evaluating us, we're evaluating the fit. Not every founder gets an engagement offer.
