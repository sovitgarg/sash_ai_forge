You have the idea, the funding, the ambition. But building AI products is a different game. The regular playbook doesn't work.

  • Can't find AI engineers

    Real AI engineers are rare, expensive, and already employed. Months of searching, no guarantees.

  • Unclear product architecture

    Which models? Which stack? How will it scale? One wrong choice now means a costly rewrite when you raise.

  • Slow, unpredictable dev cycles

    Agency timelines slip. Freelancers ghost. No one owns the outcome. Meanwhile your runway is ticking.
